Key Features



Account Manager

Master Copier Tools allows you to manage an unlimited amount of MT4 and MT5 accounts from any broker, all from one platform. There is no special software, no complex configurations and no VPS servers required.

Simply upload your account credentials to the platform and your accounts will be hosted securely in the cloud 24/7. Once you have accounts added you can utilise the platform in many ways.

ACCOUNT MANAGER





Trade Copier

The core feature of Master Copier Tools is trade copying. Our trade copier is the fastest copier in the market with an average replication speed of 50ms.

Once you have your accounts connected to the platform you can setup trade copiers. Trade copiers are highly configurable with various risk types and settings covering all scenarios.
