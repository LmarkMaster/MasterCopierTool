ACCOUNT MANAGER

Master Copier Tools allows you to manage an unlimited account of MT4 and MT5 accounts from any broker, all from one platform. There is no special Software, no complex configurations and no VPS servers required.

Simply upload your account credetials to the platform and your accounts will be hosted securely in the cloud 24/7. Once you have accounts added you can utilise the platform in many ways.

Key Features

The Dashboard

The Dashboard gives you a real time view of your accounts all in one place, removing the need to cycle through multiple terminals to manage your portfolio. You can view performance, exposure, open trades and trade history of accounts overall, or filtered down by account or specific groups.

Trade Copying

Setup trade copiers to copy trades between your own accounts or from signal accounts. Trade copiers can be setup in a few clicks and settings can be updated at any time.

Real Time Email Alerts

Trading a 24 hour market means you need to stay up to date with your activity and exposure. With Master Copier, you can setup real time email alerts for any trading activity, all with a tick of a box.

Equity Monitors

Monitor your account's equity levels and trigger an action based on a value or percentage. Whether you want to be alerted, to stop copier or have trades fully close out, Master Copier Tools makes it simple.
